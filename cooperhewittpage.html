Cooper Hewitt Design Competiton

Sketches for Cooper Hewitt's 2018 National High School Design Compeition

Sketches, research, and a first prototype of a robot concept I submitted to the Cooper Hewitt Design Competition. The robot is designed to help distribute medicine and avoid patients taking too much or too little- specifically meant to help people living alone with memory problems such as Alzheimer's or dementia.
