Lamp Project

Lamp project fall 2017 progress and final product.

In the fall semester of 2017 I designed and built a desk lamp using metal and wood. I first sketched out and designed possible ideas. I then measured out the dimensions of the lamp and created a final sketch. I cut, drilled, and glued the wood together and soldered the wiring after connecting the light.
